RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

SEC_01

ELIGIBILITY & CONDUCT

Participation is open to all residents of Kyrgyzstan aged 16 and older. Minors must provide guardian consent via the registration portal.

  • Respect all participants and mentors.
  • No plagiarism. All code must be authored during the event.
  • Zero tolerance for harassment or discriminatory behavior.
SEC_02

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

You own what you build. However, all projects submitted to the SAGA Hackathon must be licensed under an Open Source license (MIT, Apache 2.0, or GPL).

SEC_03

HARDWARE & TOOLS

Participants must bring their own machines. We provide the power, high-speed internet, and cloud credits (where applicable).

SEC_04

DISQUALIFICATION

Failure to comply with the following will result in immediate removal from the protocol:

  • Using pre-existing commercial projects.
  • Sabotaging other teams' infrastructure.
  • Submitting late without prior administrative approval.
