PARTICIPANT HANDBOOK

PLAN

Approximately 300 participants are expected to join the event. Prior to the main program, online informational sessions will be conducted on April 7–8 to provide guidance and preparation for all participants. The event will be supported by 10 mentors, who will guide teams throughout the development of their projects. In addition, 6 judges will evaluate the final presentations and select the most innovative and impactful solutions. To ensure smooth organization and coordination, around 30 volunteers will assist during the event, including 15 volunteers from the lyceum.

EVENT SCHEDULE

The program will take place over three days at different venues: Day 1 – Seytek STEM Center: Opening Ceremony and official launch of the event. Day 2 – Seytek STEM Center: Team collaboration, mentorship sessions, and project development. Day 3 – Seytek STEM Center: Final project completion and presentations to the judging panel.
